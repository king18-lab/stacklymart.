Who We Are

Stackly Grocery is a fast-growing quick-commerce company delivering groceries, pharma essentials, and daily needs within minutes. Founded with a mission to simplify everyday shopping, we combine technology, logistics, and local partnerships to redefine convenience.

Our Story

Founded in 2024, Stackly began as a hyperlocal grocery delivery service. Within two years, we expanded into pharma, fresh produce, and lifestyle essentials. Our tech-driven supply chain ensures real-time inventory and lightning-fast delivery.

Today, Stackly operates in 25+ cities with thousands of products and a growing customer base.

Headquarters

Our headquarters is located in Bengaluru, India — the technology capital of the country. From here, our operations, product development, and leadership teams coordinate nationwide logistics.

We also maintain regional hubs in Mumbai, Delhi, Hyderabad, and Chennai.
